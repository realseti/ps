Kailas Kora

Embark on the sacred pilgrimage of Kailash Kora, a trek around the sacred Mount Kailash. This revered mountain is considered the abode of Lord Shiva and a symbol of spiritual awakening. The trek offers breathtaking views, challenging trails, and a profound spiritual experience. Join us as we navigate the Kora, where each step is a step closer to the divine.

Manasarovar Lake

Nestled amidst the majestic Himalayas, Manasarovar Lake is a sacred jewel of Tibet. Revered for its spiritual significance, the crystal-clear waters mirror the towering peaks that surround it. Pilgrims believe that circumambulating this sacred lake purifies the soul. Join us on a journey to Manasarovar, where tranquility meets divinity, and every step is a pilgrimage of the heart.

Itinerary

  • Day 1

    Arrival in Lhasa (3650 m)

    Transfer to hotel. Rest, acclimatization.

  • Day 2

    Lhasa

    Jokhang Temple, Barkhor Street. Transfer to Samye.

  • Day 3

    Samye → Mindrolling → Lhasa

    Drive: 250 km. Monastery tour.

  • Day 4

    Lhasa → Lake Namtso → Lhasa (day trip)

    Drive: 440 km. Altitude: Nakchu-La Pass (5190 m), Lake (4718 m).

  • Day 5

    Lhasa → Lake Yamdrok-Tso → Gyantse → Shigatse

    Drive: 350 km. Altitude: Kamba-La Pass (4794 m). Tour of Pelkor Chode Monastery and Kumbum Stupa. Overnight in Shigatse (3900 m).

  • Day 6

    Shigatse → Saga

    Drive: 240 km. Morning tour of Tashilhunpo Monastery. Overnight in Saga (4400 m).

  • Day 7

    Saga → Tirthapuri

    Drive: 330 km. Visit to the sacred site of Tirthapuri, ritual bathing in hot springs. Overnight in Tirthapuri guesthouse (4600 m).

  • Day 8

    Tirthapuri → Darchen → Start of Kora → Dirapuk

    Short drive to Darchen. Trek: 18 km, 6–7 hours. Overnight in a guesthouse near Dirapuk Monastery (5000 m).

  • Day 9

    Rest in Dirapuk

    Meditation at the North Face of Kailash.

  • Day 10

    Dirapuk → Dolma-La Pass → Zutulphuk

    Trek: 18 km, 7–9 hours. Pass altitude: 5630 m. Overnight in a guesthouse near Zutulphuk Monastery (4790 m).

  • Day 11

    Zutulphuk → Completion of Kora → Lake Manasarovar

    Trek: 10 km, 3–4 hours. Meet with transport. Drive to Lake Manasarovar. Overnight in a lodge near the lake (4550 m).

  • Day 12

    Manasarovar

    Day of rest and integration. Free time on the lake shore.

  • Day 13

    Manasarovar

    Visit to Chiu Monastery. Practices at the lake.

  • Day 14

    Transfer Manasarovar → Border (Kodari/Purang area)

    Overnight near the border.

  • Day 15

    Border crossing. Transfer to Kathmandu

    Overnight in Kathmandu.

  • Day 16

    Free day in Kathmandu

    Departure from Kathmandu or continuation of the program in Nepal.
